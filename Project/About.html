About Nude studio

Feel & Look Fabulous At Nude Studio

Nude Studio is a warm and friendly salon located on Southall broadway, offering some of the best advanced treatments such as Fat Dissolving Injections, Laser Hair Removal, Vampire Facial & Hair PRP treatment, Bridal & party makeup, and Dermal Fillers in London.

We take pride in offering great customer service every time you visit. We also offer Massage & Body Scrub, Hairdressing, Manicure & Pedicure, and facials.

London Aesthetic Beauty offers universal beauty standards to rejuvenate your skin as never before. This is the one-stop destination for the divine salon experience. Get your own personalized treatment!

Top-Notch Quality Products & Treatment

The products that we use and offer in our salon are of the highest quality as we are proud of the performance of our work and will therefore only use “Excellent” products. Specialising in techy facials and innovative treatments, we pride ourselves on our highly professional staff and loyal customer base.

Feel free to pop in for a consultation – we look forward to welcoming you to Nude Studio.

The salon is open every day, seven days a week, all year round. The exception is January 1, 2 and 7. If clients are busy on weekdays and cannot be free until late, then you can make an appointment with the desired master at a later time or on weekends, but in advance. Administrators and foremen will also be late after the standard working hours, but this should be discussed in advance.

Highlight and support beauty with us. When planning a business meeting, party or gala dinner, start right away with a visit to the salon. Make a splash in every event.

Call and come to the salon in the near future. Or leave an online application and sign up on the site!
